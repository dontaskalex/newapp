The Teams

Players are split into two secret teams: Good Guys (want to help the company) and Bad Guys (want to destroy it). One Bad Guy is The Mole â€” the secret boss.

How to Win

  • Good Guys win: Pass 5 green cards OR find and fire The Mole
  • Bad Guys win: Pass 6 orange cards OR get The Mole chosen as Partner (after 3 orange cards)

Each Turn

  • The Boss picks a Partner
  • Everyone votes Yes or No (pass phone secretly)
  • If Yes wins: Boss sees 3 cards, picks 2 to give to Partner who plays 1
  • If No wins: Next player becomes Boss. Three No's in a row = random card played!

Special Powers

When orange cards pass, the Boss gets powers: peek at someone's team, pick the next Boss, or kick someone out of the game.
