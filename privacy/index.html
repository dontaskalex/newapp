Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

hafnr is a collection of browser-based games, tools, and experiments. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the data we collect.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about page visits to understand which tools are most useful
  • Local Storage: Some games and tools save preferences or progress locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect your IP address when fonts are loaded.

Cookies

We and our advertising partners may use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Serve relevant advertisements
  • Analyze site traffic

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.
