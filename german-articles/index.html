How It Works

Practice German grammatical gender by choosing the correct article for each word: der (masculine), die (feminine), or das (neuter).

Mastery System

Your progress is saved automatically. Each word tracks how many times you got it right or wrong:

  • Mastered = more correct than wrong answers
  • Learning = equal correct and wrong
  • Needs practice = more wrong than correct

The progress bar fills as you master more words out of the total 555.

Practice Mistakes

Click "Practice Mistakes" to focus only on words you're struggling with. Words disappear from this mode once you master them.
